AccuTerm Instructions
Open AccuTerm, and click on "Tools" in the top menu. Then click on "Settings."
On this screen, there is only one thing that needs to be changed. In the field labeled "Host Name," enter the following... 192.168.1.101
Now click on "File" from the top menu and select "Save" to save changes.
You should now be able to login normally.
